Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Piano Butler ("we", "us") operates thepianobutler.com, a free repertoire search tool for piano exam syllabuses. This page explains what information is collected when you use the site and how it is used.

1. Information we collect

  • Locally stored preferences: saved pieces and lists are stored in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Contact form submissions: if you use a contact or application form on this site, the name, email, and message you provide are processed by a third-party form service (Web3Forms) and forwarded to our inbox, solely to respond to your enquiry.
  • Standard server logs: our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and advertising partners may automatically log standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. Cookies and advertising

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Third-party services

  • Google AdSense — advertising. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube (Google) — in-page video previews. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Web3Forms — contact form processing. See Web3Forms Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, or want information you submitted via a form to be deleted, please reach out via the Contact us form on the homepage.

Piano Butler is an independent repertoire reference tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by AMEB, ABRSM, or Trinity College London.
