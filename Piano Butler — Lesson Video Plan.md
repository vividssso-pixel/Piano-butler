# Piano Butler — Lesson Video Content Plan
*Created: 2026-05-26*

---

## 1. Product Overview

**What it is:** Short expert lesson videos for AMEB piano exam pieces (Series 19 & 18), sold by grade.

**What makes it different:** Not a generic "how to play piano" tutorial. Sohyun breaks down each specific exam piece — the musical context, the technical challenges, and the mistakes students always make. This is the knowledge that usually only exists in a private lesson room.

**No sheet music on screen.** Students already own the book. Videos reference bar numbers only — no copyright issues.

## 4. Video Format

### Length
- **5–8 minutes per piece** — long enough to be useful, short enough to watch before a lesson.

### Structure (every video follows this order)

| Section | Duration | What happens |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **1. Play-through** | ~1 min | Sohyun plays the piece once, straight through. Sets the standard. |
| **2. Musical context** | ~1 min | Composer, era, style. "Why does this piece feel the way it feels?" |
| **3. Technical points** | ~3–4 min | 2–3 specific challenges demonstrated at the piano. Bar numbers called out. |
| **4. Common mistakes** | ~1 min | The 1–2 things students almost always get wrong. Shown, then corrected. |
| **5. Quick recap** | ~30 sec | One sentence summary of what to focus on. |

### What's on screen
- Sohyun's hands on the keyboard (close-up camera)
- Optionally: Sohyun's face (builds trust and personality)
- No sheet music — bar numbers mentioned verbally ("from bar 12...")

### Tone
Professional but warm. Like a lesson, not a lecture.

## 5. Production Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Proof of concept (first 4 weeks)
Make **4 videos** across 2 grades. One per week. Goal: find your rhythm, test the format.

| Week | Piece | Grade | Why this one |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Pick 1 Prelim S19 piece you know well | Prelim | Easiest to start — familiar territory |
| 2 | Pick 1 Prelim S18 piece | Prelim | Completes the pair |
| 3 | Pick 1 G1 S19 piece | Grade 1 | Step up in difficulty |
| 4 | Pick 1 G1 S18 piece | Grade 1 | Completes Grade 1 sample |

After 4 videos: show to 2–3 teacher friends. Get honest feedback before making more.

### Phase 2 — First sellable bundle (weeks 5–12)
Make remaining Prelim + G1 videos. Target: **50 videos total** across 8 weeks (roughly 6/week — adjust based on Phase 1 pace).

### Phase 3 — Grade 2–4 (ongoing)
Build out Grade 2, 3, 4 over 3–6 months.

## 8. Recording Setup (minimal viable)

You don't need a studio. You need:

- **Camera:** iPhone on a stand, positioned above/side of keyboard. 4K is fine.
- **Audio:** Zoom H1n or similar small recorder clipped near the piano. Audio quality matters more than video quality.
- **Lighting:** One ring light or softbox to the side. Avoid overhead ceiling light only.
- **Background:** Clean, uncluttered. Dark background works well.
- **Editing:** iMovie or CapCut is enough for cuts + text overlays with bar numbers.

Estimated setup cost if starting from scratch: **$150–300 AUD.**
